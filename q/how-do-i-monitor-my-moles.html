How do I monitor my moles?

· ·

I have lots of moles, what is the best way to monitor them?

It is a good idea to get into the habit of regularly checking your moles - over time you will come to learn the normal pattern of your moles and recognise when existing moles have changed or when new moles have developed. However, where you have a large number of moles it can be more difficult to recognise such changes. In this situation, it is a good idea to photograph different body sites with a phone or a camera (ask someone to help). Every few months repeat the photographs and compare side by side to check whether there has been a change in the appearance of the existing moles or whether new moles have developed. There are now a number of mobile apps that can help with this.

If you have lots of moles or your moles are irregular in appearance it may be worth having a full skin check performed by a dermatologist first to ensure that all of the existing moles are fine.
